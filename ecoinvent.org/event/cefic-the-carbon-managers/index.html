The Carbon Managers: Pathways for the EU Chemical Sector’s Transition Towards Climate Neutrality and Circularity

9 December 2024 / 13:00 / Brussels, Belgium / Participant
Register here
More details

These insights will inform the next Commission priorities, including the Clean Industrial Deal, Climate Targets for 2040, Industrial Decarbonisation, and the Circular Economy Act.

On December 9th, Cefic will unveil an extensive report presenting their findings on the iC2050 model through a detailed Base Case scenario, followed by a series of sensitivity analyses exploring different levels of ambition and enabling frameworks.

 

The event includes a keynote speech, a presentation of the iC2050 model and outcome including Q&A, a panel discussion, and some closing words by the Director General of Cefic.

 

Attending from ecoinvent, Project Manager Iasonas Ioannou will seek insights.
